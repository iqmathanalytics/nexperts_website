Frequently Asked Questions About CCNA Certification
What is a Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)?
CCNA is an associate-level networking certification offered by Cisco that validates skills in networking fundamentals, routing, switching, IP connectivity, security, automation, and enterprise networking technologies.

How to get CCNA certification in Malaysia?
To get CCNA certification in Malaysia, learners typically complete CCNA training, practice hands-on networking labs, and pass the Cisco CCNA 200-301 certification exam through Pearson VUE testing centers.

How much does CCNA training cost in Malaysia?
The CCNA Certification Training Course at Nexperts Academy costs RM 3,300 and includes the Cisco exam voucher, official courseware, rack lab access, mock exams, and instructor-led training.

What is the CCNA 200-301 exam pattern?
The CCNA 200-301 exam includes networking fundamentals, IP services, security fundamentals, automation, wireless networking, routing, switching, and troubleshooting concepts. The exam contains multiple-choice, simulation, and scenario-based questions.

Is the CCNA exam difficult?
The CCNA exam can be challenging for beginners because it covers practical networking concepts, troubleshooting, routing, switching, subnetting, and security topics. However, proper hands-on training and lab practice can significantly improve pass rates.

Can I retake the CCNA exam?
Yes. Candidates can retake the Cisco CCNA certification exam if they do not pass on the first attempt. Nexperts Academy also provides retake support under specific conditions.

Is CCNA certification internationally recognized?
Yes. Cisco CCNA certification is globally recognized and valued by employers across networking, cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and enterprise IT industries.

Who should get the CCNA certification?
CCNA certification is suitable for:
Students
Fresh graduates
IT support engineers
Network administrators
System engineers
Technical support professionals
Anyone interested in networking careers

What are the prerequisites for CCNA certification?
There are no mandatory prerequisites for the CCNA 200-301 certification exam. However, having basic knowledge of networking and IP addressing concepts can be beneficial.

Is CCNA certification enough to get a networking job?
Yes. CCNA certification can help candidates qualify for entry-level networking and infrastructure support roles such as Network Engineer, NOC Engineer, and Technical Support Engineer.

How long is CCNA certification valid?
Cisco CCNA certification is generally valid for three years from the certification date.

How to recertify CCNA certification?
CCNA certification can be renewed by:
Passing another Cisco certification exam
Completing continuing education credits
Advancing to higher-level Cisco certifications such as CCNP
