Career Opportunities After Completing CCNA Certification

After completing the Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification, learners can apply for various entry-level and mid-level networking and infrastructure roles across Malaysia and internationally.

As businesses continue investing in cloud networking, cybersecurity, wireless infrastructure, SD-WAN, and enterprise network modernization, the demand for skilled networking professionals continues to grow.

Some of the popular career opportunities after CCNA certification include:

Network Engineer

Configure, maintain, troubleshoot, and optimize enterprise networks using Cisco routing and switching technologies.

Network Administrator

Manage LAN, WAN, wireless infrastructure, IP addressing, VLANs, VPNs, and enterprise connectivity operations.

Technical Assistance Center (TAC) Engineer

Provide technical troubleshooting and support for enterprise networking environments, routers, switches, firewalls, and connectivity issues.

System Engineer

Support server infrastructure, virtualization, cloud networking, enterprise systems integration, and IT operations.

Network Operations Center (NOC) Engineer

Monitor enterprise network performance, security alerts, uptime, incident management, and network troubleshooting activities.

The CCNA 200-301 exam includes networking fundamentals, IP services, security fundamentals, automation, wireless networking, routing, switching, and troubleshooting concepts. The exam contains multiple-choice, simulation, and scenario-based questions.
