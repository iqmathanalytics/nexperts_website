Our Story

15 years in.
Then we started teaching.

Nexperts Academy didn't begin in a training centre. It began in a server room at 2am, troubleshooting a network outage for a Malaysian bank. And in a SOC operations floor during a live ransomware incident. And in a cloud migration project that went sideways for a telco, until it didn't.

After 15 years of real enterprise work — across cybersecurity architecture, cloud infrastructure, network engineering and DevOps automation — our founder Abdul Vaheed realised something uncomfortable: most of the people he hired didn't struggle because they lacked intelligence. They struggled because their training had never taught them what actually happens in production.

Nexperts Academy exists to fix that. We teach IT the way it works — in the real world, with real stakes, with real Malaysian enterprise context baked into every example.

How we teach

The method behind
the 98% pass rate.

Three things we do differently that most training providers don't — and why they matter.

01

We teach to the role, not the exam

Our courses start with the job — what does a SOC analyst actually do on day one? What does a cloud architect need to make decisions under pressure? We build the course around that, then align it to the exam objectives. When you understand the role, the exam becomes a formality. That's why 98% of our students pass first attempt — not because we drill them on answers, but because they genuinely know the subject.

02

Malaysian industry context, not Western defaults

Every lab scenario, case study and example in our courses is drawn from Malaysian enterprise environments — banking under Bank Negara oversight, telco infrastructure, government agencies operating under MAMPU and NACSA frameworks, and manufacturing under PDPA compliance. When students recognise the environment in the examples, they retain it faster. Most international training content uses generic Western scenarios that don't translate.

03

Three mock exams before the real one

Every student sits a minimum of three full timed mock exams before we let them book their real exam. Not practice questions — full exam simulations under exam conditions, reviewed question by question with an instructor. We track weakness patterns across cohorts and update our exam strategy coaching accordingly. If you're not hitting 85%+ consistently on mocks, we don't let you book yet. That's how we maintain 98%.
