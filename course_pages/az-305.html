What this course is

AZ-305 is
architecture mastery.

The Microsoft Certified: Azure Solutions Architect Expert (AZ-305) is the highest Microsoft architecture credential. It tests scenario design across identity, governance, business continuity, infrastructure and data — the cross-domain choices that real enterprise architects make.

Nexperts AZ-305 is delivered as a 6-day deep-dive against a multi-tenant Azure environment. You architect across two regulated MY case studies — a fictional bank and a fictional healthcare provider — covering all six AZ-305 design domains.

AZ-305 will not test whether you know a service. It will test whether you can choose between two services with overlapping capabilities and defend the choice.

The 2026 refresh strengthens governance, hybrid identity and Microsoft Fabric data platform alignment. We teach with current MY context — BNM RMiT, PDPA, healthcare data sovereignty and government cloud-first policy.
