What this course is

Networking is
the nervous system of IT.

Network+ proves you can design, configure and troubleshoot wired and wireless networks regardless of vendor. It's the credential most often paired with A+ to step into real network and infrastructure roles.

At Nexperts, Network+ is taught in front of an actual rack — Cisco switches, Aruba access points, a pfSense firewall and a Juniper edge router. You learn the protocol fundamentals on the whiteboard, then walk over to the rack and prove them.

You cannot troubleshoot networks from a slide deck. You troubleshoot them from a console, a packet capture and a clear head.

The N10-009 update doubles down on cloud-edge networking, SD-WAN, IPv6 and zero-trust segmentation — the things you'll actually configure in your first network role.
