What this course is

GCP PCA is
architect leadership.

The Google Cloud Professional Cloud Architect (PCA) is GCP's flagship architecture credential. It validates that you can design and operate secure, scalable, highly available cloud solutions on Google Cloud — spanning identity, networking, data, ML and migration domains.

At Nexperts, PCA is delivered against a multi-project GCP organisation with VPC Service Controls, Anthos and BigQuery pre-loaded. You architect across two MY case studies — a fintech and a healthcare provider.

GCP architecture is opinionated — Google has clear preferences about how to do things. PCA tests whether you align with those preferences. We coach the alignment, not just the services.

The 2026 update emphasises Vertex AI, Gemini integration, modern data-mesh architectures and Anthos hybrid patterns. We teach with current MY GCP deployments — banking analytics, public-sector data residency, healthcare sovereignty.
