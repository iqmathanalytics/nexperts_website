What this course is

Where Excel stops
being a glorified calculator.

Excel is the universal business tool. It is on every desktop in every MY company. The difference between an analyst and an indispensable analyst is depth in Excel — PivotTables, Power Query, Power Pivot and dynamic-array formulas. This course closes that gap.

At Nexperts, Excel Advanced Analytics is delivered as a 3-day intensive across 24 hours of hands-on lab time. We give you realistic MY business datasets (sales, finance, HR, ops) and walk every advanced feature with practical exercises. By day 3 you've built two end-to-end interactive dashboards.

Most Excel users have never touched 80% of what makes Excel powerful. Power Query alone usually saves an analyst 4-6 hours a week. We've had students pay back the course fee in saved time within their first month.

We focus on the modern Excel stack — dynamic arrays (Microsoft 365), Power Query, Power Pivot and DAX-lite. We do not waste time on macros and VBA — those tools are deprecated for new analytics work.
