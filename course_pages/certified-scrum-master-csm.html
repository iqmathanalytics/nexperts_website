What this course is

Where Scrum stops
being daily standups.

CSM is the most-recognised Scrum Master credential globally. It is delivered exclusively by Certified Scrum Trainers (CSTs), and the Nexperts CSM is taught by an active CST with 14+ years of Scrum coaching experience across MY MNCs and international product organisations.

By day 2, you've not just heard about Scrum — you've practised facilitation in role-play sprints, coached a struggling team simulation, and run all five Scrum events with feedback. The CSM exam follows the course as a 50-question online assessment with 80% pass and 90 days to attempt.

CSM is the cert most loved by hiring managers in MY — every product company, fintech and software house in Klang Valley has at least one open Scrum Master role at any given time. The cert opens that door.

The 2026 update sharpened the focus on team facilitation skills, evidence-based outcomes, and AI-augmented Scrum (Copilot in Jira, structured retros). We cover all three with hands-on practice.
