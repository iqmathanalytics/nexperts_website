What this course is

Where Power Platform
becomes enterprise architecture.

PL-600 is Microsoft's Power Platform Solution Architect credential — the senior expert-tier cert in the Power Platform family. It validates that you can lead end-to-end Power Platform delivery for enterprise customers: envisioning, architecture, security, integration, ALM and governance.

At Nexperts, PL-600 is delivered as a structured 4-day workshop on a real RFP — a multi-tenant retail customer with 3 country branches, on-prem ERP, sensitive data and a strict release governance posture. By day 4 you've designed, presented and defended the architecture for a 4,200-user Power Platform deployment.

PL-600 is the cert that filters Power Platform builders from architects. The exam tests whether you can read a 60-page RFP, ask the right 5 questions, and present an architecture that works — not just one that uses every feature.

The 2026 PL-600 update sharpened the focus on Copilot Studio architecture, Microsoft Fabric integration, Azure API Management, and the new Power Platform Pipelines. We cover all four with hands-on architecture sprints.

One scenario exam.
Heavy on case study.
