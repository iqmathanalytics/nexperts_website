What this course is

WAN transformation
you can actually run.

ENSDWI is Cisco's professional-level SD-WAN implementation course and exam path. It is the credential networking engineers pursue when enterprises retire legacy MPLS-only WANs and adopt intelligent overlays with central policy.

At Nexperts, labs use Cisco SD-WAN on-prem components with scenario overlays mimicking Malaysian enterprise realities: HQ in KL, factories in Penang and Johor, cloud SaaS breakout and LTE backup.

You will touch design (routing protocols on WAN transports), deployment (templates, device onboarding), operations (monitoring, alarms, QoS) and troubleshooting (control vs data plane paths).
