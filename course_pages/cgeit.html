What this course is

Where IT governance stops
being IT alone.

CGEIT is ISACA's executive credential for the governance of enterprise IT. It is the cert most often booked by CIOs, IT directors, audit-committee members and consultants advising boards on IT-governance maturity. CGEIT is the cert that opens the audit-committee chair conversation.

At Nexperts, CGEIT is delivered as a 4-day executive intensive. By day 4 you've defended a board-of-directors IT-governance review for a MY-listed company, designed a benefits-realisation programme, and walked an enterprise IT-strategy refresh.

CGEIT is one of the few certs read by board members who don't otherwise read certs. It is the credential most strongly recognised at the audit-committee level in MY-listed companies and government-linked entities.

The 2020+ CGEIT update aligned with COBIT 2019 governance objectives, sharpened the digital-strategy lens and integrated the BNM RMiT board-of-directors expectations.
