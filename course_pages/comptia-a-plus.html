What this course is

Every IT career
starts with A+.

CompTIA A+ is the world's most recognised starting credential for technology support roles. It validates that you can install, configure, troubleshoot and support hardware, software, mobile devices and networks across Windows, macOS and Linux.

At Nexperts Academy, A+ isn't a slide-led grind. We run real teardowns of desktops and laptops, real network troubleshooting on a live lab, and walk you through the same support tickets we see graduates solve in their first month at MNCs in Cyberjaya and KL.

A+ is not just a cert. It is the only one that proves you can sit in front of a broken machine and fix it before lunch.

The 2026 update of A+ (Core 1 220-1101 and Core 2 220-1102) adds modern troubleshooting for cloud-managed endpoints, zero-trust identity flows, and the Windows 11 administrative model. You finish able to walk into any L1 or L2 support role and be productive on day one.

Two exams. One foundation.
