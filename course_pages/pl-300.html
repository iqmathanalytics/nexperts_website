What this course is

PL-300 is
the BI hire credential.

PL-300 (Power BI Data Analyst Associate) is the most-requested data certification in the Malaysian enterprise market. It validates that you can prepare data, model it, visualise it, analyse it, deploy it and maintain it inside Power BI.

At Nexperts, PL-300 is delivered against three real-shaped MY datasets — a banking transactions extract, a hospital admissions view, and a retail point-of-sale feed. You build executive-grade reports for each.

The hardest part of Power BI is not DAX. It is choosing what to put on the page so the exec acts on it. We coach that, not just the formulas.

The 2026 update covers Microsoft Fabric integration, Copilot for Power BI, and modern enterprise deployment with deployment pipelines. We teach all three with current MY enterprise BI deployments.
